# Business Context — Distill Brief

Fill in each field, then paste the completed file into `/think` at the start of a session.
Distill will remember your context for future sessions and use it to ground every skill.
Be honest — not how you wish the business ran, but how it actually runs today.
Delete the instructions and leave only your answers. Partial context is fine.
